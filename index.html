Projects
Sketch modeling. 2022

This is my implementation of the feature strokes extraction and graphics generation rendering for 3D sketch modeling according to a SIGGRAPH paper [Li et al.]. I interpreted the stroke rules in the paper,designed the interactive interface and the post-processing flow of the depth and normal map.

[News] Later, I expanded this program into a small system with a multi-client-server architecture for collaborative scene building in the Unity engine
